○ ABOUT US ○

Akshay Heaters is one of the most prominent Indian companies involved in the manufacturing of industrial heating elements. Well known for our quality and excellence, our dominance is unquestionable. Since our inception in 1993 in the industrial town - Aurangabad, Maharashtra, we have been highly successful in supplying quality assured products. Our specialty lies in making tailor made products as per the demands of the customers.

Quality, excellent service and customer satisfaction come foremost to us and our unwavering dedication has helped us a lot to arrive at this stage. Valuable feedback, inputs and consistent support of our customers has contributed immensely in our overall growth.

○ Infrastructure ○

A robust infrastructure is the foundation to real growth. Following the same adage we have a solid, well-equipped production unit that facilitates manufacturing of international quality industrial heaters. Our production units are manned by a team of highly skilled and efficient professionals. More so, our company is armed with the latest communication gadgets.

Production Process

Reliability, prompt deliveries and competitive pricing are guaranteed. Our multi-functional air heaters are patented worldwide and conformed to CE, CUL, TUV and GS safety standards.

We believe that the quality of products and services benefits from a good organization of the operational activities and from well structured decision processes. We obtained ISO 9001:2000 and ISO 13485 certifications in 2004, comply with current regulations for both products and quality management systems.

Awards and Certifications
